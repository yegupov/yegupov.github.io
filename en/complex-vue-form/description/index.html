Complex form in Vue.js

Task:

Make up a complex form with tabs and accordions with response validation and error message output.

The form is in the Offers section. Fields to fill out are in the General, Targeting groups and Payouts tabs.

Technologies and plugins used:

  • Vue.js (Vue CLI, Router, Vuex)
  • Ckeditor.js — Rich-text editor
  • Simple-Vue-Validator — fields validation
  • V-chips-multiselect — select with multiple choice
