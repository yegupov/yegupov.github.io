  4. Dropdown menu after login. The Exit button should open and work.
  5. Offer of the week. here the sroll should work along the arrows to the right and left. By clicking, 1 new offer opens. In terms of content, we use 3 images from the design, they just have to change one after another.
  6. Mobile layout. In a real project it will be, but in a test one it is not needed.
